This test measures some aspects of task switching, and also takes about 10 minutes to complete.

You will see 1 of these arrows on the computer screen

You must pay attention to both the colour and direction of the arrow.

If you see a blue arrow, you should press the direction that the arrow is pointing using the up and down arrow keys.

If you see a red arrow, you should press the opposite direction that the arrow is pointing.

There will be a cross in the centre of the screen, and the targets will appear in place of the cross.

Please try to keep your eyes fixed on the cross during the test.

As mentioned earlier, you must pay attention to the arrows, and indicate the direction it is indicating by pressing the up or down arrow keys on the keyboard.

Remember, Red arrows indicate the opposite direction.

This test measures both your reaction time and your accuracy, so it is important to respond as quickly as you can, but without making too many errors.

To facilitate quick responding, keep your index finger over the up arrow (8 on the num pad), and your thumb on the down arrow (2 on the num pad).

There will be no break or practice during this Trial.

The trial will take approximately 15 minutes.

Please tell the test supervisor if you are ready to begin.
